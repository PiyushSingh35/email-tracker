How Tracking Works

Pixel Tracking: An invisible 1×1 transparent GIF is appended to every email. When the recipient opens the email, their mail client loads the image from your server, logging the open.

Link Tracking: All links in the email body are wrapped in a redirect through your server. When clicked, the server logs the click and forwards the recipient to the original URL.

Sender Exclusion: Opens and clicks from your IP addresses (configured above) are silently ignored so your own activity never inflates the counts.
